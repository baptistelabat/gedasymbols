Microcontrollers

ATMega 100pin uC
This symbol is 4 parts. Assign them all the same refdes and they appear as one symbol to the other gEDA parts.
  • ATMega640-1280-2560_ADC.sym - ADC busses F,K; AVCC AREF and AGND
  • ATMega640-1280-2560_IO1.sym - I/O busses B,E,G,H,L
  • ATMega640-1280-2560_IO2.sym - I/O busses A,C,D,J
  • ATMega640-1280-2560_pwr.sym - VCC, GND, !RESET and XTAL
