Notes

All tools, symbols and footprints are licensed under GPL 3 for distribution and are unrestricted for any use.

Most footprints are specifically designed for home-made, hand-drilled PCBs: They have large or oval pads and small drill-holes that make the drill bit position itself nicely, thanks to the copper thickness.

Tools

Python scripts to generate DIP and jumpers/headers footprints, optionally with oval pads. Still work in progress, they could be improved a lot. The result is visible in my footprints section.

  • createDIP.py: Generate DIP footprints
  • createHeader.py: Generate Jumper and Header footprints
