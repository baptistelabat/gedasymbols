Titleblock

  • titleblock.sym - A titleblock with detached attributes for title, author, date, revision, etc.
  • in.sym - input port symbol for sub sheets in a hierarchy
  • out.sym - output port symbol for sub sheets in a hierarchy

Analog

  • opamp.sym - generic single operational amplifier
  • opamp_pwr.sym - power symbol for the generic opamp
  • opamp_dual.sym - generic dual opamp
  • opamp_dual_pwr.sym - power symbol for dual opamp
  • opamp_offset.sym - opamp with offset compensation at pin 1 and pin 4

Discretes

  • cap.sym - vertically drawn generic capacitor
  • diode.sym - generic diode
  • zener.sym - generic zener
  • res.sym - N-channel enhancement IGBT
  • res.sym - generic resistor, DIN EN style
  • res2.sym - generic resistor, ANSI style
  • nmosfet.sym - generic n-channel MOSFET
  • nmosfet_power.sym - generic n-channel MOSFET with body diode
  • pmosfet.sym - generic p-channel MOSFET
  • pmosfet_power.sym - generic p-channel MOSFET with body diode

Power

These symbols provide a specific net
  • gnd - ground
  • vcc - positive logic supply
  • vee - negative logic supply
  • Ub+ - positive analog supply
  • Ub- - negative analog supply
  • 5V+ - +5 V
  • nc - deliberately not connected

Specific

  • photodiode_BPW34.sym - photodiode BPW34
