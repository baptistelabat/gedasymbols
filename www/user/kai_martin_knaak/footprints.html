template pcb

The template.pcb may be used to start drawing footpints with the pcb GUI. Layers and sizes are set to reasonable default values.

generic SMD

These footprints are meant to supersede the ones from the default library. They are designed to meet the requirement of densely populated boards with too little space outside the elements to place the refdes or value. Pad size is sufficient to hand solder the parts.
