generic SMD

These footprints are meant to supersede the ones from the default library. They are designed to meet the requirement of densely populated boards with too little space outside the elements to place the refdes or value.
  • 0805.fp - 0805 with refdes/value on the pads
  • 1206.fp - 1206 with refdes/value on the pads
  • 1812.fp - 1812 with refdes/value on the pads
  • SO8.fp - SO8
  • QSOP14.fp - QSOP14
  • QSOP16.fp - QSOP16
