Untested

  • TC7WU04F - Three invertors in one package (Toshiba).
  • TLC7226CN - Quad 8-bit Digital to Analog converter (Texas Instruments).

  • STA013T - MP3 decoder IC, TQFP44 package (STMicroelectronics).
  • MAS3507D - Another MP3 decoder chip, PLCC44 only, obsolete (Micronas Intermetall).
  • CS4331 - dual 18-bit A/D converter, serial interface (Cirrus)
  • 8726 REC - Standard REC chip used in Commodore 17xx REUs (Commodore/MOS).
  • 8722 MMU - Standard MMU chip used in Commodore 128/128-DCR (Commodore/MOS).
  • 41256 - Common 41256 256 kbit x 1 DRAM chip.
  • 44256 - Common 44256 256 kbit x 4 DRAM chip, DIP package only.
  • HM51440A - 1Mb x 4 DRAM chip, for ZIP-20 package (Hitachi).
  • VBT1-S5-S12-SMT - 5v to 12v DC-DC converter (83mA output), probably works for most of their other single-output modules as well, judging by their datasheet (V-Infinity).

Footprints

Common Parts

Tested/working on a board

  • Basic non-polarized capacitor - 200-mil lead spacing, commonly for small ceramics, polyester film, etc.
  • Resistor/Diode - 100-mil lead spacing for folded-over 1/8W resistors and small diodes like 1N914. Also *just* fits 1/4W resistors. Not for 1N400x-type diodes (leads on these are usually too thick).
  • Common TO-18 - For 2N2222 or similar, derived from Eagle part.

Untested

  • Four-in-one resistor network - 0603 (x4) surface mount isolated resistor pack (Yageo YC164 series).
  • PTS625SL50-LFS - SPST momentary switch, normally open, low-profile pushbutton type (C&K Components).
  • Common Mounting hole - A simple screw hole, 350 mil pad diameter, 130 mill drill (as a component so PCB won't move it around).
  • PT10LH01 - Common potentiometer - standing upright, horizontal shaft (Piher)
  • DIP 12 - Simple 12-pin DIP package.
  • Fuseholder - Common 5mm diamater fuse holder; two separate clips joined into one footprint (Littelfuse).
  • 2-pad jumper - Surface mount style jumper pads, 40 mil square.
  • 3-pad jumper - Surface mount style jumper pads, 40 mil square.

Connectors

Tested/working on a board

  • Male 44-pin card edge - 100 mil pin spacing, Suitable to plug into Commodore 64/128 "Expansion Port" connector.
  • Male 44-pin card edge, no silk - (derived from the above, without the silk layer so it can be connected to from either edge)
  • Male 44-pin card edge, short - (derived from above, even shorter (useful as test points, or to mount something at the edge of a board).
  • Female 44-pin edge connector - 100 mil pin spacing, 200 mil row spacing, with silk added to mark "keepout" areas around it to match Commodore 64 "Expansion Port".

Untested

  • 2-794954-0 - Female ATX Connector, as used on most mainboards. 20-pin version, from datasheet (Tyco/AMP).
  • 2-1586041-0 - Female ATX, Right angle, 20 pin version, from datasheet (Tyco/AMP).
  • Female 5-pin DIN - PCB mounted, suitable for older keyboards, etc. derived from Eagle part.
  • Female 15-pin D-Sub - Standard female VGA connector, derived from Eagle part.
  • Female 15-pin D-Sub (short) - Shortened version suitable for use at the edge of a board, similar to PCB's other D-Sub connectors.
  • Female 44-pin edge connector - Typical, 100 mil pin spacing, 191 mil row spacing.
  • Male 24-pin card edge - 156 mil spacing, suitable to attach a Commodore 64 "User Port" connector to.
  • PG203J - Basic 3.5mm stereo jack (the kind Radio Shack sells), derived from Eagle part.
  • SJ13533NG - Lower-profile 3.5mm stereo jack, without internal switches, based on datasheet (CUI, Inc).
  • 163-7620-E - 5.5mm outer contact, 2.1mm pin diameter (Kobiconn).
  • DC0202 - Female DC Power jack. Generic footprint, derived from Eagle part.
